Think about what baking means to you: maybe it's a recipe passed down in your family that carries the flavors of a particular culture or place; maybe it's a memory of someone who baked for you when you were growing up; maybe it's something you've started making as an adult that has become a ritual or an act of connection.

It could be tied to a holiday tradition, a specific ingredient, a technique your grandmother swore by, or even a total disaster that somehow became a favorite story.

Come ready to share — and if you have a recipe that holds a special place, bring that too. There's no wrong answer, no competition; just the stories behind the food.
